About us

Who we are

Located in McConnelstown, PA, Heritage Baptist Church (HBC) is a small independent church with a heart for spreading the gospel of Jesus Christ. The church is led by our Pastor Lou Zannino (on the right) and Associate Pastor Dennis Allison (on the left).

The church’s vision is “To encourage and equip families to honor Jesus Christ; to cultivate a lifestyle of worship; to creatively reach every generation with the Gospel; and to instill in every heart a passion for missions.”
